ATXMega32E5 - low cost, TQFP-32

  • 18 general purpose I/O, 2 serial, TWI, SPI
  • 12 possible PWM outputs
  • 8 dedicated A:D on PORTA, 8 additional A:D on PORTD
  • Differential A:D (with gain)
  • Complex Waveform generation and Analog Comparator interrupt
  • 32k of NVRAM for application, 4k for boot loader
  • Costs less than ATMega328 as TQFP-32
