Advantages of XMega processor

  • Runs at 32mhz with INTERNAL clock - no crystal/resonator needed
  • Separate 'boot' section (more memory for application)
  • Lower cost - ATXMega32E5 costs less than ATMega328 (TQFP-32)
  • At least 2 serial ports available
  • Additional I/O pins with more pin 'modes'
    'Totem pole', open drain/source, pull up/down, edge/level
  • 12-bit A:D with differential A:D conversion and adjustable gain
  • Advanced interrupt support (3 levels), any pin can be an interrupt
  • More pins can be set up as PWM outputs
